Back pain is one of the most common complaints, often tied to posture, stress, or muscle strain rather than anything serious. Gentle, consistent care tends to help more than aggressive intervention for everyday stiffness.

**When to see a doctor:** Sudden severe pain, pain radiating down a leg, numbness, or pain following an injury should be evaluated by a medical professional. This content is educational and not a substitute for professional medical advice.
